## Small Unit Resupply Decision Dominance (SUR-DD)

Objective: Accelerate resupply planning and execution through automation.

## Tasks:

- Create/manage geolocated vehicle inventory and monitor current/future usage
- Create/manage vehicle performance models
- Automate resupply vehicle selection based on availability and mission suitability (eg weight/volume and range)
- Integrate vehicle tracker and automatically identify takeoff, successful landing, and/or mission failure.  Notify personnel of events only.
- Extract the dismounted unit's route and monitor their current location.
- Select resupply date, time, location based on vehicle specifications (flight duration) and dismounted unit's route, pace, and current position; allowing for  mobility by hitting a moving target.
