## (U) WHAT IS AMMOSAN AND WHY?

AmmoSAN Integrates various disparate/ standalone efforts into a reliable and operationally effective capability, resulting in:

- Realtime Cl-V (ammo) consumption/supply level sensing
- Call for resupply
- Cl-V (ammo) supply data and resupply demand data aggregation
- Pre-flight resupply planning, coordination, and tasking
- Automatic long range / 'Last Tactical Leg' resupply execution:
-  Optimized target selection based on vehicle capability & environment
-  Optimized target selection based the unit's route/pace
-  Automatic resupply vehicle selection and preflight data sourcing & distribution
-  Digital supply manifesting and vehicle tasking
-  Automatic resupply event detection, notification, and retasking (for mission failure)
- Shared resupply mission profile (sustainment & tactical unit)
- Informed predicted logistics
